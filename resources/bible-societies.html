Resources

Bible Societies

Inclusion of a site in this list is not an endorsement or an acknowledgment of the correctness or spirituality of the site. To submit a site or leave a comment click here.

Translation and Distribution

  • American Bible Society

    Work with their partners to make the Bible available and alive for people in the most difficult circumstances. Translates and distributes Bibles in many countries.

  • Bible Gateway

    Read, hear, and study Scripture at the world's most-visited Christian website. Grow your faith with devotionals, Bible reading plans, and mobile apps.

  • Bible Society in Australia

    The goal of Bible Society Australia is the same one that Bible Societies around the world have been pursuing for some 200 years: to translate, publish, distribute and engage people with the Bible.

  • United Bible Societies

    The United Bible Societies (UBS) is a worldwide association of Bible societies.

  • Bible Society of Maine

    Distributing Scriptures at cost and through grants to qualifying ministries.

  • One Hope

    Our mission is to affect destiny by providing God's eternal Word to all the children and youth of the world.

  • Canadian Bible Society

    Publishes and distributes the Bible in Canada and around the world. Translation into native Canadian languages, news articles, newsletter.

  • Danish Bible Society

    The Danish Bible Society is a not-for-profit church-based organization which publishes the authorized Bible in Danish and Greenlandic on an ecumenical basis.

  • Digital Bible Society

    Making God's Word Accessible in Every Language. Free online Bibles and resources.

  • Foreign Language Christian Resources

    An Online Catalog containing Scriptures in over 80 languages, Christian books in about 45 languages, the Who is Jesus? tract, Bible-based ESL materials, the JESUS DVD in over 1100 languages, plus a variety of foreign-language Christian DVDs.

  • GOD'S WORD to the Nations

    Our mission is to identify and empower individuals to take GOD’S WORD to the English-speaking people of the world, focusing especially upon those who do not know Jesus as their Savior. We produce outreach tools—Scripture portions and complete Bibles—and promote the biblical outreach strategy: “Become a Seed Planter and Change Lives.”

  • Interactive Bible Study (HK)
  • Maryland Bible Society

    Slowing down to read the Bible. Listen as Dave Moyer and Josh Mitchell discuss studying God's Word.

  • National Association of State and Regional Bible Societies

    Coordinating agency for independent, local Bible Societies within the United States.

  • National Bible Association, Incorporated

    Sponsor of National Bible Week and encouraging everyone to read the Bible.

  • Scripture Union

    Committed to helping children, young people and their families know God's love, follow Jesus and meet God daily in His Word.

  • SGM Lifewords

    Our passion is to see the Bible freely shared with others – changing lives and helping people to come to know Jesus for themselves. We produce a range of literature resources, develop mission programmes for marginalised communities, and pioneer new ways for people to explore the Bible in their own language and culture.

  • Slavic Gospel Association

    Our purpose is to help the evangelical churches make disciples of the people of the lands of Russia for our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ through prayer, strategic ministry and financial assistance.

  • The CrossWire Bible Society

    The CrossWire Bible Society is an organization with the purpose to sponsor and provide a place for engineers and others to come and collaborate on free, open-source projects aimed at furthering the Kingdom of our God.

  • Trinitarian Bible Society

    Founded in 1831 for the circulation of Protestant or uncorrupted versions of the Word of God.

  • BibleLeague International

    Serving the under-resourced Church globally through the provision of Bibles, biblical resources, and training to transform lives worldwide through God’s Word.

  • Wycliffe Bible Translators of Canada

    Linking the Canadian Church with the world's minority language groups, to see community transformation through Bible translation, use of translated Scriptures, mother-tongue literacy and education.

  • Wycliffe Bible Translators UK

    Wycliffe Bible Translators believe that the Bible is the best way for people to come to know and understand who God is. Of the 6,887 languages in the world today, only 554 have a complete Bible.

  • Wycliffe Bible Translators USA

    For more than 70 years, Wycliffe has helped people around the world translate the Bible into their own languages. We also help with language development, literacy and other spiritual and physical needs.

Translation Only

  • Lutheran Bible Translators

    We make God's Word accessible to those who do not yet have it in the language of their hearts.

  • Pioneer Bible Translators

    To evangelize by Bible translation, literacy, and using scripture to all people in the language they understand.

More Online Resources

  • BibleNet USA

    BibleNetUSA is the national information site for BibleNets, which are community networks of volunteers promoting Bible reading.

  • Cambridge University Library - Bible Society's Library

    The British and Foreign Bible Society (now "Bible Society") was founded in 1804 with the aim of supplying Bibles and New Testaments - Scriptures - without note or comment, on a world-wide basis, in a language people could understand and at a price they could afford.
